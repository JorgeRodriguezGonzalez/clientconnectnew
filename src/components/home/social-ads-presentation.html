Facebook Ads Strategy

For Client Connect Australia

Marketing Agency Built by Tradies, for Tradies

1Hyper-Segmented Conversion Campaigns by Trade Type

Create separate campaigns for each niche:

  • Pool builders
  • Fence contractors
  • Pergola builders
  • Bathroom renovators
  • Deck builders
  • Landscapers
  • Roofers
  • Carpenters

Detailed Targeting:

  • Interests: Business management, Small business, Entrepreneurship
  • Behaviors: Small business owners
  • Pages they follow: Material suppliers (Bunnings Trade, Reece, etc.), tradie associations
  • Job titles: Owner, Director, Manager + specific trade type

2Implement the Pixel and Track Key Events

Set up conversions for:

  • Free audit request
  • Case study download
  • Call scheduled
  • Contact form completed

Proper tracking is essential to identify which audiences and creatives are actually driving qualified leads, not just clicks.

3Creatives That Speak THEIR Language

Your Unique Advantage:

You're still tradies who run a roofing business - you know their real pain points because you're living them

Winning Creative Angles:

"We're tradies just like you. Started as home builders and chippies, now we run a roofing business. A few years back, we spent $15K with a marketing agency that had never been on the tools. Three months later: 2 shithouse leads.

Now we help tradies like us get 20-30 qualified leads per month - while still running our roofing company. Because we understand your client isn't just anyone - whether it's someone with $15K for a new roof or $80K for a pool, we know how to find them."

Alternative angle:

"We're not ex-tradies who left the tools behind. We're home builders and carpenters who now run a roofing business AND help other tradies get leads. We learned marketing because agencies kept ripping us off. Now we do both - and we only work with tradies because we ARE tradies."

Content Formats

  • Video testimonial: You or your partner on site at your roofing job or in the workshop - showing you're STILL tradies (authenticity is everything)
  • Before/after carousel: Show real client results (lead dashboard, ROI, closed projects)
  • Video case study: "How we helped [Name] Fencing go from 3 to 18 jobs per month"
  • Meme/text image: "Your marketing agency: 'We need to do brand awareness' / You: 'Mate, I need jobs for next week, not likes on Instagram'"

4Trade-Specific Landing Pages

Don't send everyone to the same page. Create:

  • clientconnectaustralia.com.au/pool-builders
  • clientconnectaustralia.com.au/roofers
  • clientconnectaustralia.com.au/bathroom-renovators

Specific Language for Each:

  • Pool builders: "Leads who actually want to spend $50K-100K on a pool"
  • Roofers: "Homeowners ready to spend $10K-25K on a roof replacement, not tyre kickers"
  • Bathroom renovators: "Qualified leads for $15K-40K bathroom renos"

Include:

  • Testimonials from OTHER tradies in that specific industry
  • Case studies with real numbers
  • "We're still tradies running our own roofing business" founder story

5Identify Which Type of Tradie Converts Best

After 3-4 weeks, analyze:

  • Do pool builders have better LTV than roofers?
  • Which industry has the best close rate?
  • Which age ranges/locations convert best?
  • Are certain trades more profitable for you?

This data will inform where you double down your ad spend and which trades to prioritize in your scaling efforts.

6Powerful Retargeting with Social Proof

Create Audiences Of:

  • Those who watched 50%+ of your founder story video
  • Visitors to specific case study pages
  • Engagement with posts about "agencies that don't understand tradies"
  • People who've interacted with your "still on the tools" content

Retargeting Ads:

  • Video testimonials from tradie clients in different industries
  • "Free audit of your current ads - we'll show you where you're losing money (we've seen it all, we're tradies too)"
  • "We're still running our roofing business while helping other tradies get jobs. Here's how."
  • Detailed case study in downloadable PDF

7Scale with Advantage+ Towards Lookalikes

Once you identify your best clients (e.g., pool builders in QLD with $70K average ticket, or roofers in NSW doing insurance work), create:

  • Lookalike 1-3% of paying clients
  • Lookalike of clients with best LTV
  • Lookalike segmented by trade type
  • Advantage+ campaigns that find more like them

Advantage+ uses machine learning to find users similar to your best converters across all Meta properties (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, Audience Network), optimizing automatically towards quality conversions.

8Organic Content That Builds Authority

Post content that proves you KNOW the tradie life because you're LIVING IT:

Content Ideas:

  • "3 mistakes fence contractors make on Facebook Ads (and how we fix them)"
  • "Why your current agency doesn't understand the difference between a $5K deck job and a $60K pool project"
  • Short videos from your roofing jobs showing you're still on the tools
  • "What we learned about marketing while still running our roofing business"
  • Behind-the-scenes of you at a roofing job in the morning, then reviewing a client's leads in the arvo
  • "Red flags your marketing agency has never worked with tradies" (e.g., they talk about 'brand awareness' when you need jobs next week)
  • Photos/videos in work gear with caption: "Morning: on the roof. Afternoon: getting our clients more roofing jobs"

9Test Creative Angles Around Your Unique Story

Founder Story Angles to Test:

"We're home builders and carpenters who now run a roofing business. And yeah, we also do marketing for tradies."

"Most marketers have never held a spirit level. We're literally on roofs every week. That's the difference."

10Monthly Creative Testing (20+ Variations)

Test combinations of:

  • Different "still on the tools" angles
  • Industry-specific pain points
  • Client testimonials (video + text)
  • Results screenshots (actual dashboards, lead notifications)
  • Memes about marketers who've never been tradies
  • Educational content about lead quality vs. quantity
  • Behind-the-scenes of your roofing work + marketing work

Constant creative testing is essential. The market fatigues quickly, and what works this month might not work next month. Always be testing new angles.
