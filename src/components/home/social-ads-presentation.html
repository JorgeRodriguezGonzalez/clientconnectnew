3 Creatives That Speak THEIR Language

Your Unique Advantage:

You're still tradies running a roofing business - you know their real pain points because you're living them

Winning Creative Angle:

"We're tradies just like you. Started as home builders and chippies, now we run a roofing business. A few years back, we spent $15K with a marketing agency that had never been on the tools. Three months later: 2 shithouse leads.

Now we help tradies like us get 20-30 qualified leads per month - while still running our roofing company. Because we understand your client isn't just anyone - whether it's $15K for a new roof or $80K for a pool."

Creative Formats

  • Video testimonial: You on site at roofing job - showing you're STILL tradies (authenticity is everything)
  • Before/after carousel: Real client results (lead dashboard, ROI, closed projects)
  • Video case study: "How we helped [Name] Fencing go from 3 to 18 jobs per month"
  • Meme/text image: "Your marketing agency: 'We need to do brand awareness' / You: 'Mate, I need jobs for next week, not likes on Instagram'"

Constant creative testing is essential. Test 20+ variations monthly to avoid ad fatigue.

5 Identify Which Trade Converts Best

After 3-4 weeks, analyze:

Client Quality:

  • Which industry has best LTV?
  • Which has best close rate?
  • Most profitable trades for you?

Demographics:

  • Which age ranges convert best?
  • Which locations perform better?
  • Time of year patterns?

  • People who interacted with "still on the tools" content

Retargeting Ads:

  • Video testimonials from tradie clients in different industries
  • "Free audit - we'll show you where you're losing money"

7 Scale with Advantage+ & Lookalikes

Once you identify best clients (e.g., pool builders in QLD with $70K average ticket), create:

Lookalike Audiences:

  • 1-3% of paying clients
  • Clients with best LTV
  • Segmented by trade type

Advantage+:

  • ML finds similar users
  • Auto-optimizes to quality
  • Across all Meta properties

Advantage+ uses machine learning to find users similar to your best converters, automatically optimizing towards quality conversions.
